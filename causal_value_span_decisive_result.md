## Executor Read

The decisive comparison does not support the hypothesis that single-token
causal value-span geometry alone improves value grounding in this setup. RAW
and CAUSAL are behaviorally identical on all 24 rows under deterministic
decoding. The protected FORCED-CEILING still recovers 11/12 exact arguments on
both slices, confirming that the schedule/candidate stack can force the desired
values, but that is a protected result and not a promotion signal.
